What you may and may not take from here.

The method, so you can run it on us

Exploration and crystallisation. Learn, rinse, repeat.

This site was built the way its source papers were: explore an idea space fast, crystallise what holds, publish with the seams showing, and let contact with better-informed people melt the weak parts. That method produces real errors and real gold, usually adjacent. The register system exists so you always know which risk you are taking — and the licence exists so that when you find gold, you can take it home, non-commercially, without asking.
