The boundary, stated exactly

What you may and may not take from here.

From gold panels: quotes you can verify at the linked source in under a minute, and dated status claims meant to be re-checked, not trusted. From purple panels: patterns to argue with. The author's documents "have errors and are rapid iteration attempts at fleshing out the idea space" — his words, kept here on purpose. They are gold seams and cracks, not gospel; treat any figure inside them as a provocation to check.

If you ever find a purple claim dressed in gold — a proposal presented as settled fact — that is the most serious category of error this site recognises. Report it and it will be fixed within the day, and logged below.

A third, smaller register runs through the site in ember: culture — song doorways from the author's own music. Songs are neither record nor proposal; they are the resonance layer, each names its maker, and none claims any voice but his own.

The method, so you can run it on us

Exploration and crystallisation. Learn, rinse, repeat.

This site was built the way its source papers were: explore an idea space fast, crystallise what holds, publish with the seams showing, and let contact with better-informed people melt the weak parts. That method produces real errors and real gold, usually adjacent. The register system exists so you always know which risk you are taking — and the licence exists so that when you find gold, you can take it home, non-commercially, without asking.
