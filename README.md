# Native Nations of the World — Sovereign Co-Evolution Lab

A forkable, static, offline-friendly lab in the P4A cinema family, addressed to native nations of the world: Indigenous data sovereignty, decentralised compute, reciprocity economics (the C-Hour) and the long road toward revised agreements — offered as pathways to consider, never directions to follow.

**Live site:** https://auraofintelligence.github.io/p4a-native-nations-cinema/

## The two registers

Everything on the site is colour-coded by provenance, and never mixed:

- **Gold panels — the established record.** Real instruments, quoted exactly and cited: UNDRIP, FPIC, ILO Convention 169, the Nagoya Protocol, the WIPO treaty on genetic resources, the CARE Principles, the First Nations principles of OCAP®, the Maiam nayri Wingara principles, Te Mana Raraunga, Local Contexts TK & BC Labels, and a world-edition treaty atlas.
- **Purple panels — P4A proposals.** Rapid iterations from one workbench: the L0–L3 sovereign compute gradient, the Community-Hour (a receipt for care, mobilised never monetised), and a kintsugi protocol for repairing broken agreements. They contain errors by the nature of their speed. React, revise, refuse — all three are welcome.

## Standing commitments

- This site never speaks for any nation, and lists no nation except by that nation's own hand.
- Free, prior and informed consent is treated as minimum viable respect — including the right to refuse.
- Any nation's entry is published verbatim, and removed on request without process.
- No analytics, no accounts, no cookies, no forms. Static pages only.
- Correction of anything, for any reason, is one message away: open an issue.

## Self-onboarding

Nations and peoples not visible here can add themselves entirely on their own terms — see the [onboarding room](https://auraofintelligence.github.io/p4a-native-nations-cinema/pages/onboard.html), the entry template inside it, and [ONBOARD_WITH_AN_AGENT.md](ONBOARD_WITH_AN_AGENT.md) for building your own version with your own AI assistant. Declining is a first-class outcome.

## Design lineage

Next chapter of the P4A cinema system — [p4a-xyz-cinema](https://github.com/auraofintelligence/p4a-xyz-cinema) (the Australian workbench) and [p4a-oceania-cinema](https://github.com/auraofintelligence/p4a-oceania-cinema) (the regional lab) — with a kintsugi theme: gold seams as the literal visual system for showing repairs, breaks and provisionality. Plain HTML/CSS/JS, self-hosted variable fonts, progressive enhancement (fully readable with JavaScript off), reduced-motion fallbacks, SVG-only artwork for low bandwidth.
